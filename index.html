Recent Projects

Packaging for Client

Step and Stone make handmade bakery produce with a social purpose, representing the capabilties of those with learning disabilities.

Me and my team produced luxury packaging for their Lavosh and Biscotti produce, each with shelf ready trasport options.

Design For Manufacture- Radio

For this assignment, I was given a portable FM radio. Tasked with producing a working prototype shaped to user needs, it was essential to ensure each component from the original product would fit within the assembly.

SharkNinja Cocoa-Falls

A valentines project briefed by SharkNinja, 'Cocoa Falls' is a spin-off design inspired by chocolate fountains. Melted chocolate is put into the heating funnel, which travels through the machine to a motorised auger. Chocolate is pushed up to the top causing a waterfall to pour out over the dipping wall. Includes a heart shaped mold for excess waste.

Eco-Puff

After analysing the continuing growth of single-use vape pens and the internal components (battery, chemicals, ect.) I discovered that there is currently no enviromentally safe methods of product disposal. Therefor I produced a sustainability focused design with 'disassembly' and 'repair' as priority factors.

SnapTracker

This branding project taught me the importance of a tailored design to company values. Branding builds company reputation and markets products. I chose 'SnapInc' the creators of Snapchat for my project and designed a GPS tracking device that displays location anywhere in the world using the Snap Maps feature.

Pour-tea-ble

My kettle redesign incorporates in-depth research into user experience. User empathy tools and journey mapping highlighted functionality issues. I implemented solutions to simplify refilling, distrubute load and for eliminating strain on the user when pouring.
